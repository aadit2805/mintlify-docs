Drift replaces the single-point guesses of traditional budgeting apps with a full probability distribution. Connect your bank, describe a goal in plain English, and Drift runs 100,000 Monte Carlo simulations—each one modeling a plausible future shaped by income swings, inflation, emergencies, market returns, and debt—to tell you the exact odds you'll get there. Whether you're saving for a house, paying off loans, or building an emergency fund, Drift turns "I think I can" into a number you can act on.

## How Drift is different

<Note>
  Most financial apps show you a single projected balance. Drift shows you the **distribution of thousands of possible futures**—so you understand not just what's likely, but how much risk you're carrying.

  - **Account-aware modeling:** when you connect via Plaid, Drift models each credit card with its actual APR and each loan with its real amortization schedule, not generic averages.
  - **Automatic sensitivity analysis:** after every simulation, Drift tests how your probability changes if income shifts ±10%, spending shifts ±10%, or you extend your timeline by six or twelve months.
  - **What-if scenarios:** explore targeted changes—like cutting a spending category or taking a second income—and see the revised probability instantly.
</Note>

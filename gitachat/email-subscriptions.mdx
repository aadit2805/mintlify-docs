The GitaChat email subscription delivers one Bhagavad Gita verse to your inbox every morning at 8:00 AM in your local timezone. Each email includes the verse in English, an AI commentary, and a link to read the full traditional interpretation on GitaChat — a few minutes of quiet reflection before the day begins.

## Subscribing

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to the Daily Verse page">
    Visit [gitachat.org/daily](https://gitachat.org/daily).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open Settings to subscribe">
    Navigate to [gitachat.org/settings](https://gitachat.org/settings) and find the **Daily Verse Email** section. Toggle the switch on to subscribe. GitaChat uses your Clerk account email, so no separate email entry is required.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set your timezone">
    Once the toggle is on, a timezone selector appears. Choose your timezone from the list — emails will arrive at **8:00 AM** in that timezone each day.
  </Step>
  <Step title="You're subscribed">
    Your subscription is active immediately. The first email will arrive the following morning at 8:00 AM in your selected timezone.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What you receive

Every daily verse email includes the following:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Verse of the day" icon="book-open">
    The full English translation of today's verse, displayed as a pull-quote for clear, focused reading.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Chapter and verse number" icon="hash">
    The exact chapter and verse reference — for example, *Chapter 2, Verse 47* — so you can look it up in any edition.
  </Card>
  <Card title="AI commentary" icon="sparkles">
    A short reflection written by GitaChat's AI, connecting the verse's teaching to everyday life and modern experience.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Link to full commentary" icon="arrow-up-right">
    A **Read more on GitaChat →** link that takes you directly to the verse page, where you can read the traditional commentary and bookmark or share the verse.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

The email is formatted in plain, readable HTML and includes a plain-text version for email clients that prefer it.

## Unsubscribing

You can stop receiving emails at any time using either of these methods:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Option 1 — Unsubscribe from an email">
    Open any daily verse email and click the **Unsubscribe** link at the very bottom of the email. You will be taken to a confirmation page at [gitachat.org/unsubscribed](https://gitachat.org/unsubscribed) confirming that you have been removed from the list.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Option 2 — Unsubscribe from Settings">
    Go to [gitachat.org/settings](https://gitachat.org/settings), find the **Daily Verse Email** section, and toggle the subscription switch off. The change takes effect immediately.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

In both cases, the unsubscribe is immediate — you will not receive any further emails after confirming.

<Info>
  Your email address is used only to send the daily Bhagavad Gita verse. GitaChat does not share your email with any third parties, and you will never receive marketing emails unrelated to the daily verse subscription.
</Info>

## Managing your timezone

Emails are sent at **8:00 AM in the timezone you select**. To update your timezone:

1. Go to [gitachat.org/settings](https://gitachat.org/settings).
2. In the **Daily Verse Email** section, open the timezone selector.
3. Choose your new timezone from the list.

The change is applied automatically — no need to re-subscribe. Your next email will arrive at 8:00 AM in the updated timezone.

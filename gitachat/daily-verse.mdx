The Daily Verse feature gives you a moment of reflection every day — a single verse from the Bhagavad Gita, chosen for the day, presented with both AI contextual commentary and the traditional interpretation. It's a gentle way to build a daily practice with the Gita's wisdom without needing to search for anything.

## Viewing the daily verse

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to the Daily Verse page">
    Visit [gitachat.org/daily](https://gitachat.org/daily) directly, or click **Daily** in the navigation bar from anywhere on the site.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Read the verse">
    The page loads today's verse automatically, including the chapter and verse number, the English translation, and commentary. No search required.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Explore the commentary">
    Each daily verse includes both **For You** (AI contextual commentary) and the **Traditional Commentary** — the authentic scholarly interpretation of the verse. Read both to get the full picture.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save or share">
    Use the lotus icon to bookmark the verse to your collection, or use **Share** to copy a direct link to the verse page.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
  Daily verses rotate through all 700 verses of the Bhagavad Gita, so each day brings a different teaching. The verse is determined by the current date in your local timezone.
</Info>

## Email subscription

You can receive the daily verse directly in your inbox every morning — no need to remember to visit the site.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Daily Verse page">
    Go to [gitachat.org/daily](https://gitachat.org/daily).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find the subscription form">
    Scroll below the daily verse to find the email subscription section.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your email and subscribe">
    Type your email address and click **Subscribe**. GitaChat will add you to the daily mailing list.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Each morning email contains:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="The verse" icon="book-open">
    The full English translation of the day's verse, formatted clearly for easy reading.
  </Card>
  <Card title="AI commentary" icon="sparkles">
    A contextual reflection written by GitaChat's AI to help you connect the verse to daily life.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Chapter and verse" icon="bookmark">
    The exact chapter and verse number so you can find it in any edition of the Gita.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Link to full commentary" icon="arrow-right">
    A direct link to [gitachat.org/verse/chapter/verse](https://gitachat.org) where you can read the traditional commentary and use all verse features.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Unsubscribing

You can stop receiving emails at any time — there is no lock-in.

- **From any email:** Click the **Unsubscribe** link at the bottom of any daily verse email.
- **From Settings:** Go to [gitachat.org/settings](https://gitachat.org/settings), find the **Daily Verse Email** section, and toggle the subscription off.

<Tip>
  If you want to pause emails temporarily, unsubscribe and re-subscribe later — your preferences are saved and re-subscribing takes only a few seconds.
</Tip>

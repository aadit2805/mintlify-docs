The Daily Verse feature gives you a moment of reflection every day — a single verse from the Bhagavad Gita, chosen fresh for you, presented with AI contextual commentary. It's a gentle way to build a daily practice with the Gita's wisdom without needing to search for anything.

## Viewing the daily verse

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to the Daily Verse page">
    Visit [gitachat.org/daily](https://gitachat.org/daily) directly, or click **Daily** in the navigation bar from anywhere on the site.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Read the verse">
    The page loads today's verse automatically, including the chapter and verse number, the English translation, and an AI commentary tailored to bring the verse's teaching to life. No search required.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save or share">
    Use the lotus icon to bookmark the verse to your collection, or use **Share** to copy a direct link to the verse page.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
  GitaChat picks a verse you haven't encountered before, drawing from the full collection of 700 verses. Once you've seen every verse, it cycles through the collection again — so each day continues to bring a fresh teaching.
</Info>

## Email subscription

You can receive the daily verse directly in your inbox every morning — no need to remember to visit the site. Subscriptions are managed from the [Settings page](/gitachat/settings).

Each morning email contains:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="The verse" icon="book-open">
    The full English translation of the day's verse, formatted clearly for easy reading.
  </Card>
  <Card title="AI commentary" icon="sparkles">
    A contextual reflection written by GitaChat's AI to help you connect the verse to daily life.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Chapter and verse" icon="bookmark">
    The exact chapter and verse number so you can find it in any edition of the Gita.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Link to full commentary" icon="arrow-right">
    A direct **Read more on GitaChat →** link that takes you to the verse page where you can explore further.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Unsubscribing

You can stop receiving emails at any time — there is no lock-in.

- **From any email:** Click the **Unsubscribe** link at the bottom of any daily verse email.
- **From Settings:** Go to [gitachat.org/settings](https://gitachat.org/settings), find the **Daily Verse Email** section, and toggle the subscription off.

<Tip>
  If you want to pause emails temporarily, unsubscribe and re-subscribe later — re-subscribing takes only a few seconds from the Settings page.
</Tip>

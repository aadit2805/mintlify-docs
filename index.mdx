Drift is your personal financial probability engine. Connect your bank accounts, describe a goal — "save $50,000 for a house in 3 years" — and Drift runs 100,000 Monte Carlo simulations to show you exactly how likely you are to succeed, what could derail you, and how small changes improve your odds.

## Get started in three steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Connect your bank">
    Link your accounts through Plaid for real data, or use the Capital One Nessie sandbox to explore without connecting real accounts.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set your goal">
    Type or speak your financial goal in plain language. Drift's AI parses it into a simulation — no forms, no spreadsheets.
  </Step>
  <Step title="See your probability">
    Review your success probability, explore what-if scenarios, and talk to your AI financial advisor about the results.
  </Step>
</Steps>
